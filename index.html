Ruangrawee (Kao) Kitichotkul

 

I am a Coterm student (B.S. + M.S.) at the Department of Electrical Engineering, Stanford University. My B.S. advisor is Prof. John Pauly, and my M.S. advisor is Prof. Mert Pilanci.

I am interested in statistical signal processing, machine learning, and computational imaging. Since the summer of 2020, I have been working with Stanford Computational Imaging Lab, where I am mentored by Prof. Christopher Metzler and Prof. Gordon Wetzstein.
