Ruangrawee (Kao) Kitichotkul

 

I am a 2nd year PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Boston University. I work at the Signal Transformation and Information Representation (STIR) group, advised by Prof. Vivek Goyal.

I am generally interested in statistical signal processing and computational imaging. My most recent projects at the STIR group include developing models and methods for single-photon imaging and particle beam microscopy.

Previously, I was a Coterm student (B.S. + M.S.) at the Department of Electrical Engineering, Stanford University. My B.S. advisor is Prof. John Pauly, and my M.S. advisor is Prof. Mert Pilanci. At Stanford, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Christopher Metzler and Prof. Gordon Wetzstein at Stanford Computational Imaging Lab.
